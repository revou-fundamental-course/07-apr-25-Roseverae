About Us

Sinar Sosro is the first ready-to-drink tea company in Indonesia, established in the 1940s. We are commited to delivering the best tea flavor to all generations. With iconic products like Teh Botol Sosro, we have become a household name and continue to innovate with new offerings.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading and trusted ready-to-drink beverage company in Indonesia and the world.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality products
  • Focus on innovation and sustainability
  • Continuosly improve customer satisfaction
